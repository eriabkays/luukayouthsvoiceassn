About Us

Our Vision

Luuka Youths Voice Association is a community-driven organization dedicated to youth empowerment, education, and sustainable development.

Founded in 2021, we've impacted over 500 young people through various initiatives.

Our community organization has recently carried out a range of impactful activities, including mobilizing volunteers for clean-up campaigns and neighborhood improvement, visiting underprivileged schools to donate learning materials and inspire students, and partnering with local churches to support youth fellowships, community prayers, and charitable initiatives—all aimed at fostering unity, education, and positive change in our community.
